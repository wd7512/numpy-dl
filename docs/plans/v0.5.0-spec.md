### src/numpy_rl/reinforce.py (in rl/)
- REINFORCEAgent class:
  - __init__(state_dim, action_dim, hidden_dim=64, lr=1e-3, gamma=0.99)
  - policy_net: Sequential(Dense→ReLU→Dense→Softmax)
  - select_action(state): sample from policy distribution
  - store_transition(state, action, log_prob, reward): store trajectory
  - train_step(): compute returns, policy gradient, update
  - train(env, episodes): full training loop
- Creator references: Williams (1992) "Simple statistical gradient-following algorithms for connectionist reinforcement learning"
- Math: ∇J(θ) = E[∇log π(a|s;θ) * G_t]
  where G_t = Σ_{k=t}^{T} γ^{k-t} * r_k
